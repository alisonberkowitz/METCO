• METCO

    Metropolitan Council for Educational Opportunity

    • About METCO

    The Metco Program is funded by the Commonwealth of Massachusetts. It serves to reduce racial isolation in the suburbs, and provide better educational opportunities for students who would otherwise attend under-performing schools in Boston and Springfield.

    Busing Students from the City to the Suburbs

    For this project, I chose to demonstrate the adversity METCO students face in two forms. I placed the 37 school districts on a map, showing the long commutes these students make every day, some over an hour. I also chose to display the racial demographics of each school district involved (click on the markers to see), to suggest the difficulties of being a racial minority in these suburban school districts. While METCO students face adversity because of their different geographical location than most of the students they go to school with, they also are usually some of the only students of color in an otherwise mostly all white suburban school district.
