    Busing Students from the City to the Suburbs

    For this project, I chose to demonstrate the adversity METCO students face in two forms. I placed the 37 school districts on a map, showing the long commutes these students make every day, some over an hour. I also chose to display the racial demographics of each school district involved, to suggest the difficulties of being a racial minority in these suburban school districts. While METCO students face adversity because of their different geographical location than most of the students they go to school with, they also are usually some of the only students of color in an otherwise mostly all white suburban school district.
